Project Overview

Lubumbashi Eats & Stays is a local directory website designed to solve challenges in Lubumbashi by listing restaurants and hotels with detailed information, directions, and customer support guidance. The site will generate revenue by charging $1 per client for businesses that acquire clients through the platform.

  • Goal: Help residents and visitors easily discover, navigate, and contact local businesses.
  • Problem solved: Lack of a centralized, trustworthy source for restaurant and hotel information in Lubumbashi.
  • Monetization: $1 per client fee for each business successfully contacted via the site.
  • Target users: Tourists, locals, expats, NGOs, restaurant and hotel owners.
  • Value: Saves time, provides accurate directions, ensures updated info, and offers guidance support.
