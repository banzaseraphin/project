Project Overview

Lubumbashi Eats & Stays is a local directory website designed to solve challenges in Lubumbashi by listing restaurants and hotels with detailed information, directions, and customer support guidance. The site will generate revenue by charging $1 per client for businesses that acquire clients through the platform.

  • Goal: Help residents and visitors easily discover, navigate, and contact local businesses.
  • Problem solved: Lack of a centralized, trustworthy source for restaurant and hotel information in Lubumbashi.
  • Monetization: $1 per client fee for each business successfully contacted via the site.
  • Target users: Tourists, locals, expats, NGOs, restaurant and hotel owners.
  • Value: Saves time, provides accurate directions, ensures updated info, and offers guidance support.

User Scenarios

These scenarios describe typical ways our target users would interact with Lubumbashi Eats & Stays:

  1. Tourist searching for a restaurant: A visitor wants to find a restaurant near their hotel that serves local cuisine and has customer reviews. They search, filter by cuisine, check directions, and contact the restaurant through the site.
  2. Hotel owner tracking inquiries: A hotel owner wants to list their property on the site and track incoming client inquiries. They log in, update their listing, monitor messages, and respond to customer requests efficiently.

Typography

Fonts were chosen for readability, hierarchy, and modern style:

  • Heading Font: Poppins — applied to all headings (h1, h2, h3, etc.) for emphasis and visual hierarchy.
  • Body Font: Inter — applied to paragraphs, lists, captions, and general text for clean readability.
